How to Compress Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a moment
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression helps reduce file sizes, which leads to:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Improved SEO rankings
  • Better user experience
  • Lower storage requirements
